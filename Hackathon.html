About MultiLingual

MultiLingual is a website that promotes the learning of diversity and equality for students and educators across the globe!


MultiLingual aims to teach kids how to treat others well and respect each others cultural differences. In our site, students will learn the different cultures of different ethnic groups. They will learn how different and unique each race is and how no one is truly different. Multilingual also has it's own device that helps filter out any inappropriate and insensitive statments made about a person's essay. Helping it to appear honest and respectful to ethnic groups
